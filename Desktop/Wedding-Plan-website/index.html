What we do

and how we do it...

BASIC PLANNER PACKAGE

Our month of wedding planning package is the perfect option for couples who already have their wedding plans in place but want someone to take the reigns in the month leading up to the wedding and oversee everything on the day of. About 4 weeks before your wedding day, we will take over all communication with your vendors and create a detailed schedule of events from having breakfast sent to your room in the morning until DJ sets down. We will be there to coordinate your wedding rehearsal and talk you and your bridal party through every aspect of your wedding day so that you know what to expect. On the day itself, we are on hand to oversee and manage your team of professionals, making sure all the special details are as they should be, allowing you to relax and enjoy your wedding with nearest and dearest.

Our offer

COMPLETE PLANNER PACKAGE

Our full service wedding planning package is designed for those of you not in a position to be as hands on when it comes to planning your wedding. Perhaps you live abroad or have an extremely demanding job, this is potentially the right choice for you. Our full service planning package means that we are with you every step of the way, from scouting, shortlisting and securing a venue on your behalf to putting the last of your guests in a taxi home. The Complete Planner Package is ideally suited to busy couples who just want someone to take care of everything. We work with you to shape a budget, choose a collaborative team of carefully selected suppliers, style your wedding and manage the day onsite.

Our offer

WEDDING STYLING & DESIGN

Our bespoke design and wedding styling package is aimed at the couple who already have all of the logistical elements of planning in place but need help bringing the unique look and feel of their wedding to life. We begin with a design consultaion where we look at your wedding venue in detail and the various spaces available. After getting a feel for how you generally like to celebrate and the aesthetic you are both naturally drawn to, we create a bespoke look book that is essentially a visual representation of our proposed design. Included are colour palettes, fabric suggestions, room layouts, feature design elements and new ideas for floral and stationary design. The Lookbook is accompanied by a blueprint that outlines the creative vendors and services that are required to make your dream a reality.

Amazing Ireland Destinations

Ireland hosts a plethora of gorgeous, rustic venues that would make for the most magical of wedding locations. Some of our favourite fairy-tale venues include Lough Eske Castle (Donegal), Lough Cutra Castle (Galway), and Bantry House (Cork). These regal country manors offer magnificent views across both beautiful forested areas and scenic coastal points.

Wedding in the exotic Caribbean

The Wedding Specialists from Yes Wedding Agency agree that Punta Cana in the Domican Republic would be an excellent setting to have your Destination Wedding. With powder white sand beaches and aqua waters it is truly one of the most beautiful unspoiled locations in the Caribbean. Punta Cana can handle the small intimate weddings or the largest group wedding possible and anything in between.

Wedding in Empordà Castle Spain

Nothing is more romantic than getting married in a Renaissance palace or a medieval castle. Feel like royalty on that particular day by saying “I Do” at any historic places throughout the country. From a 13th century castle to a 16th century one with world-class ceilings, everything is possible in Spain. If you want to celebrate, there really is no better place than Spain.
